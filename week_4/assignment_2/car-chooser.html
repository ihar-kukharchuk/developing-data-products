Overview

It is a supportive presentation for ‘Car Chooser’ project which is intended to be used for helping in choosing the car procedure based on two characteristics:

  • horsepowers per 1000 pounds
  • miles per galon

For demonstration purposes considering application is based on mtcars dataset from ‘data’ library
